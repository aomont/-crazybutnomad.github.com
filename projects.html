MY PROJECTS










Biking across Canada

This is an idea that I'd had with my friends Clément and Sarah for a few years, and when we all graduated, we had the whole summer to ourselves - and so we went for it!

For 60 days, we biked between 90 and 185 kilometres every day ; we camped along the way, in people's gardens or backyards most of the times, on campgrounds when we couldn't find a welcoming house.

We started from Montreal, and headed East, through the Maritimes, towards Halifax, which we reached after 12 days. Then, we flew over to Vancouver ; from there, we pedalled 4 000 kilometres back to Montreal.

WHY NOT 100% ?

Short on time and resources, e didn't bike through Newfoundland and Labrador. The 18-hour ferry ride was just too much to handle.

Additionally, in the middle of Ontario, the highway we were biking on had no shoulder, and this made biking very dangerous and uncomfortable. We had to take the bus to bypass that section.

We hope we'll be able to complete the missing sections in the future.










HEC Startup Weekend & SnapRide

Startup Weekend is "a global grassroots program of active and empowered entrepreneurs who are learning the basics of founding startups and launching successful ventures."

At the beginning of the event (Friday evening) people pitch startup ideas and then form teams around the best projects ideas. 52 hours they have to present a Minimum Viable Product, business model, customer acquisition model, development roadmap, etc. in front of local established entrepreneurs who serve as jury.
